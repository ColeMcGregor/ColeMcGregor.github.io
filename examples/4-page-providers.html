Provider FAQ (example)

Keep answers short. This reduces repetitive front-desk questions.

Can I request a specific provider?

Yes. Mention your preferred clinician when scheduling. Availability varies by schedule and visit type.

Will I always see the same clinician?

Many visits can be scheduled with the same clinician, but urgent scheduling needs may require flexibility. Ask staff about options.

How do I choose the right visit type?

If you’re unsure, describe what’s going on. Staff can recommend the correct appointment type. See booking examples on the Services page.

Do providers offer telehealth?
