New patient steps

Keep this short and practical. The goal is to reduce calls and remove uncertainty.

1) Reach out

Phone · email · form

Use the contact methods below. Tell us whether you’re a new patient and what you’d like help with.

2) Share basics

Reason for visit · availability

We’ll ask a few quick questions so we can schedule the right visit type and provider.

3) Prepare for your visit

What to bring

Bring your insurance card (if applicable), photo ID, a medication list, and any relevant records you have.

Not sure what to book? Describe what’s going on and we’ll route you to the right visit type. Booking Examples

What to bring

Example checklist. Adjust to match your clinic’s policies and workflows.

Bring these items

  • Photo ID
  • Insurance card (if using insurance)
  • Medication list (name + dose if possible)
  • Allergies and prior diagnoses (if known)
  • Any recent labs, imaging, or referral notes (if relevant)

Optional but helpful

  • Prior primary care provider name + contact
  • Preferred pharmacy information
  • Brief timeline of symptoms (for sick visits)
  • Questions you want answered during the visit
