New patients

3 quick steps

  1. Call or send a message to request your first appointment.
  2. Complete intake paperwork (online or in-office).
  3. Bring ID, insurance card, and a current medication list.

If you have prior records, bring what you have—we’ll help fill in the gaps.

Returning patients

Refills · follow-ups · scheduling changes

  • Scheduling: Call during business hours.
  • Refills: Allow 48–72 hours when possible (policy varies).
  • Updates: Let us know if your insurance or contact info changes.

For urgent symptoms, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.

Before your visit

Reduce surprises

  • Confirm the clinic is in-network for your specific plan.
  • Confirm your copay and deductible status.
  • Bring your insurance card and photo ID.

If you’re unsure what to ask, we can help you verify the right details with your insurer.
