Example Website Structures

These examples demonstrate different website sizes and structural approaches. Content is intentionally generic and simplified for demonstration purposes — final websites are always customized to the specific business and use case.

The goal of these examples is to help visualize scope and organization, not to showcase visual design or final wording.

Medical Websites
