Business

McGregor Systems LLC is my contract software engineering work: stepping into existing systems, finding what’s failing, and making improvements without forcing expensive rewrites or disrupting day-to-day operations.

Services

Fix what’s broken (and keep it fixed)

Software repair · stabilization · reliability

When a system is fragile, inconsistent, or “nobody wants to touch it,” I diagnose the root cause and repair it in-place. The goal is not just a patch — it’s reducing the chance the same issue returns.

Make workflows predictable

Clear ownership · fewer surprises · less confusion

I turn unclear or inconsistent processes into dependable technical workflows — so the system behaves the same way every time, and people know what happens next.

Automation and internal tools

Reduce manual effort · improve data quality

I build lightweight tools and automation that remove repetitive steps, reduce errors, and make the day-to-day smoother.

Reports and document generation

PDFs · operational reports · client-ready output

I design and stabilize reporting/document systems so outputs are accurate, readable, and suitable for long-term reference.

Integrations using what you already have

Platforms · third-party services · low-code extensions

I connect and extend the tools you already use, reducing double-entry and helping data flow safely between systems.

System structure and architecture (when needed)

Boundaries · maintainability · clean interfaces

When a system’s structure is what’s causing the pain, I’ll help define boundaries and patterns that keep future work sane — without over-engineering.

How I deliver

Incremental changes

I avoid “rewrite first” thinking. Changes are scoped, testable, and aimed at removing the real source of failure.

Clarity + documentation

I add the minimum structure and notes that reduce future ambiguity: diagrams, handoff notes, and practical docs.

Respect for operations

Work is done with attention to the fact that your system is live and people depend on it.
