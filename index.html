I fix broken software and messy workflows.

If your tools feel fragile, your process relies on “that one spreadsheet,” or you’re stuck babysitting reports and systems that keep breaking — I can step in, diagnose what’s happening, and make it reliable again.

I work with small businesses and teams that don’t have time to untangle technical problems — but still need things to run clean.

How I Help Proof & Examples

Common situations I help with

“It works… until it doesn’t.”

Recurring bugs · brittle logic · surprise breakage

Things break when one small change happens, and nobody is confident touching it. I stabilize the system, reduce failure points, and leave it easier to maintain.

Manual work that shouldn’t be manual

Copy/paste workflows · spreadsheets as glue · preventable mistakes

If the business runs on repetitive steps and “tribal knowledge,” I can automate the right parts, add guardrails, and make outcomes predictable.

Reports, PDFs, and client documents that keep breaking

Layout issues · missing images · pagination bugs · data inconsistencies

I design and repair document/report generation so your outputs are consistent, readable, and safe to rely on long-term.

Systems that don’t talk to each other

Disconnected tools · duplicate entry · inconsistent records

I connect what you already use, reduce double-entry, and help you move data safely between systems.

How it works

1) Quick call

15 minutes

You tell me what’s going wrong and what “done” looks like. If it’s not a fit, I’ll say so.

2) Discovery

1–3 hours (usually)

I trace the issue, map the moving parts, and propose a plan. This is the moment uncertainty turns into clarity.

3) Fix + handoff

Targeted changes

I implement repairs/improvements, add guardrails where needed, and leave behind notes/documentation so you’re not stuck later.
