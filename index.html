Engagement Focus

McGregor Systems LLC is the umbrella for my contract software engineering work. I take on engagements where a team needs someone who can understand an existing system quickly, make meaningful improvements, and leave it more maintainable than they found it. This typically involves stepping into live systems, diagnosing issues in place, and making targeted changes without disrupting ongoing operations.

Delivery Approach

I work by clarifying workflows and constraints, identifying failure points, and making incremental, well-scoped changes. Fixes are applied close to the source of the problem, with structure and documentation added where they reduce ambiguity and future friction.

About Me

I’m a systems-focused developer with experience spanning service-oriented architectures, application development, and performance-conscious simulation work. I care about code that stays understandable over time, has clear interfaces, contains intentional tradeoffs, and is constructed with design patterns and principles that don’t collapse under change.

My background includes U.S. Air Force service and formal software-development training. I tend to be most valuable where a project needs structure: establishing architecture, untangling complexity, improving reliability, and leaving behind systems other engineers can work with confidently.
