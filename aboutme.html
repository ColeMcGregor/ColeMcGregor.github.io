About me

I’m a systems-focused developer. I do best when a project needs structure: untangling complexity, improving reliability, and leaving behind something other people can work with confidently. I love the challenge of making even the most complex system into simple to understand, use, and maintain.

Working style

I aim for practical changes with clear impact. I like small steps that move a system from “fragile” to “predictable”: better boundaries, fewer hidden dependencies, clearer ownership, and less surprise breakage.

Translation: I’m not here to rewrite everything you have, I’m here to make it work. I can help even with other people's less than ideal code.

Background

My background includes U.S. Air Force service and formal software development training. I have experience across backend services, desktop tooling, and system-heavy application work, including reporting pipelines and automation.

B.S. Software Development (Cum Laude) - Siena College · A.S. Computer Science - HVCC

Principles I Bring to Projects

Clarity over cleverness

Readable code, obvious data flow, and fewer “mystery behaviors.”

Fix near the source

Patch symptoms only when necessary; otherwise remove the real cause of failure.

Leave things better

Documentation and structure where it meaningfully reduces future confusion.
